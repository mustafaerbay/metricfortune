Display shipping costs earlier in checkout
43% of your customers are abandoning their carts at the shipping calculation step. This suggests that unexpected shipping costs are causing friction at a critical moment in the purchase journey.
Recommended Action
Show estimated shipping costs on the cart page, before users begin checkout. This transparency helps set expectations early and reduces abandonment at the final step.
Implementation Steps
1. Add shipping calculator to cart page
2. Display estimate prominently above "Checkout" button
3. Include message: "Final shipping calculated at checkout"
4. Test on mobile and desktop
